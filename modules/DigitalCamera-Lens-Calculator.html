Depth of Field & Equivalence Formulas

Hyperfocal (H): (fÂ² / (N * c)) + f
Near Limit: (D * (H - f)) / (H + D - 2f)
Far Limit: (D * (H - f)) / (H - D)
Total DoF: Far Limit - Near Limit
Variable Key:
  • f: Focal Length (Actual)
  • N: F-Number (Aperture)
  • D: Subject Distance (Fixed 2m)
CoC (c):

Circle of Confusion. Calculated as 0.030 / Crop Factor. This adjusts for the required magnification of the sensor format.
